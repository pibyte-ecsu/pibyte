About the Pi Byte Club

For Your Knowledge

The Pi Byte Club of Elizabeth City State University is an academics centered club of the within the Mathematics and Computer Science Department of Elizabeth City State University. The club serves the interests and goals of the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science through offering an environment for tutoring, networking, and learning with a focus on the the fields of mathematics and computer science.

About the Website

As the official website of the Elizabeth City State University Pi Byte Club, this website holds information concerning upcoming and past events, the cabinet and advisors, the alumni of the university; resources regarding jobs, scholarships, and grants; contact information associated with the Pi Byte Club of Elizabeth City State University. The website is subject to regular updates and is the responsibility of the members of the Pi Byte Club or chosen affiliates to maintain.
